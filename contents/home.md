
#### Biography
I am currently a visiting student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). Starting from September, I will be pursuing a Ph.D. in Statistical Mathematics at SUSTech under the supervision of Professor [Haoran Zhang](https://hrzhang16.github.io/). My research interests encompass various aspects of network analysis and psychometrics.

In network analysis, my work focuses on longitudinal networks and signed networks, where I study the dynamics and relationships within complex systems over time. Additionally, I am deeply interested in psychometrics, particularly in latent variable models and techniques like multidimensional scaling/unfolding, which are used to explore the underlying structure of psychological data.

Through my research, I aim to develop new methods and insights that contribute to our understanding of these fields and advance the applications of statistical mathematics.
